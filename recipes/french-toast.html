French Toast Recipe

Description

French toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and typically milk, then pan fried.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 teapoon ground cnnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Thick bread slices

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of flour into a large mixing bowl
  2. Add 1 cup of milk
  3. Add 3 eggs
  4. Add a pinch of salt
  5. Add 1 teaspoon of ground cnnamon
  6. Add 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  7. Add 1 tablespoon of sugar
  8. Whisk this mixture together until it's smooth
  9. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or heavy frying pan over medium heat
  10. One at a time, soak slices of thick bread in the batter until it's saturated
  11. Cook each piece of bread until it's golden brown on both sides
